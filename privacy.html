Privacy Policy

Rest Pulse — Heartbeat Simulator

Effective date: 2026-02-08

Overview

Rest Pulse is designed for relaxation and mindfulness. We respect your privacy and aim to keep data collection to a minimum.

Data We Collect

Rest Pulse does not require an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email, address, or health data.

Ads (Google AdMob)

The app may show banner ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect limited device information to serve ads and measure performance. We configure ad requests to request non-personalized ads (NPA) where applicable.

Note: Ad serving and measurement are handled by Google’s SDK. Please review Google’s privacy information for details.

Analytics

Rest Pulse does not use custom analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not build user profiles.

Permissions

Rest Pulse uses system haptics on iPhone and Apple Watch. The app does not access HealthKit.

Children’s Privacy

Rest Pulse is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
